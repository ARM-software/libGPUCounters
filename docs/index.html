Performance Counter Reference Guides

Arm Mali and Immortalis GPUs provide a wide range of performance counters to help you understand your application's performance characteristics, and to find behavioral inefficiencies to optimize.

These performance counter reference guides provide a complete listing of all performance counters that can be sampled or synthesized for most shipping Arm GPU products.

Note that many products share a single underlying counter guide, which occurs in cases where there are multiple GPU products that share a common hardware architecture.
