Arm GPUs

Performance Counter Reference Guides

Arm® Mali™ and Immortalis™ GPUs provide a wide range of performance counters. You can use them to understand your application's performance characteristics, and to find behavioral inefficiencies to optimize.

These guides provide a complete listing of all performance counters that can be sampled or synthesized on supported Arm GPU products.

Note

Many products share a single counter guide, which occurs in cases where the products that use a common hardware architecture.
